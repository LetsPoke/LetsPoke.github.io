About Me

Hello and welcome! My name is Marcel, born in '92, and currently living in Berlin, Germany. In 2019, I decided to study International Media Computer Science at the University of Applied Sciences for Engineering and Economics (HTW) in Berlin and successfully completed my studies with a Bachelor of Science in October 2023.

Originally, I started my studies with the goal of becoming a game developer. However, over time, I discovered my enthusiasm for backend development and data analysis, which brought me significantly more joy. Currently, I am on my way to becoming a Full-Stack Developer with a focus on backend development. I am particularly fascinated by the analysis of user data and enjoy collaborating with data teams.
